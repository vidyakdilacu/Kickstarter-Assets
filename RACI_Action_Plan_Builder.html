Module 5 — RACI Action Plan Builder

Three steps to a working action plan. Choose initiatives, name owners, build the RACI matrix. Output: a screenshot you can take back to your team and execute on.

⏱ 45-60 min 🔒 Private 📷 Screenshot to share

Step 1 · Pick the initiatives that matter for your organisation

Browse the cards below. Click to select. Aim for 3 to 10 initiatives. Once selected, set a horizon (3, 6, or 12 months). Skip cards that don't apply.

i
Tip: The first horizon (3 months) should be your highest-priority initiatives. Don't try to do everything at once. Most successful programs ship 3–4 initiatives in the first quarter.
Filter:

Step 2 · Pick the roles who will own the work

Choose 3 to 6 roles that exist in your organisation. After selecting, type the actual person or team name — abstract roles become real commitments when you write a name.

i
Tip from Microsoft Learn: If you cannot agree on who fills a role, that itself is the finding — it usually means the role is shared across teams in a way that creates accountability gaps. Capture it on a follow-up list.

Step 3 · Build the RACI matrix

For each initiative, click the R/A/C/I button under each role to indicate their involvement. Multiple letters per cell are allowed.

i
Tip: Start with R (who's doing the work). Then A (who's accountable). Then C and I. Goal: exactly one A per row. Multiple A's dilute accountability.
